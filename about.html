ABOUT VIRIDE

Viride is the brainchild of Ameya Patil and Vivek Shome. 'Viride' is Latin for green, and describes the objective of this initiative. We work to return the hills of Pune to their natural green splendour.

Students volunteer to hike and map previously unknown trails, and help clean up the area by picking up the accumulated trash and sending it off for recycling.
